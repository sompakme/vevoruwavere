How often should caffeine eye creams be applied, and when?

"Caffeine eye creams can be applied once or twice per day, or just at night depending on other ingredients in its formulation," Murphy-Rose explains. "For example, retinol should only be used once a day under the eyes before bedtime."

Is caffeine best for treating under-eye puffiness compared to other skincare ingredients?

Caffeine is a go-to among skin experts, and one of the best and most effective ingredients to target under-eye puffiness. Other top ingredients also include arnica and vitamin K.

What are some signs that may indicate an eye cream is irritating to skin?

According to Murphy-Rose, signs of irritation include redness, itching, burning, swelling, peeling, roughness, and watery eyes. "Be aware that contact hypersensitivity reactions are delayed, so symptoms may develop five to seven days after exposure to an offending product," she advises.
