The Boys Season 2 trailer, release date and news

Superhero film lovers can, without a doubt, recall the superhero team, The Seven, from the debut season of The Boys. The Boys are the rivals of The Seven. The two rival teams face each other as The Boys attempt to control the corrupt members of The Seven. The series premiered on the 26th of July 2019. Even before the premiere of this season, The Boys Season 2 had already been approved for filming.

The first season of The Boys had intense and relevant themes that pleased the audience. The Boys Season 2 is a continuation of the first season. It is a fantastic superhero series that Eric Kripke developed for Amazon Prime Video. It is based on the comic book series, which are written by Garth Ennis and Darick Robertson.

What happens before The Boys Season 2

The Boys episodes in the first season ended with critical revelations and unexpected twists. Although fans were aware of the rivalry between Billy and Homelander, the final episodes in the first season reveal that Billy abhors superheroes. He particularly loathes Homelander because he raped his wife, Becca, who was formerly working for Vought. In the finale, Becca is alive and is a mother to the child she conceived from the rape ordeal. Billy is broken when he realises that his wife is raising a child whose biological father is his arch-nemesis.
